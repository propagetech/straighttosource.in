Soft Line Product Inspection

Thorough checks for fabrics and textiles, from construction and colour to seams, labelling, and packing.

Soft Line Product Inspection

What we check

  • Checking the GSM or GSF of fabric as per the buyer's requirement.
  • Counting the number of threads (the construction of the fabric).
  • Comparing colours (of fabrics, accessories, and stitching threads) with a TPX or TCX Pantone book, lab dips, or fabric swatches.
  • Checking colour fastness to washing, by washing a sample on a normal cycle together with a piece of white cotton fabric.
  • Checking dry colour fastness, by rubbing with a piece of white tissue cotton fabric.
  • Making sure there is no colour shading on the same product, and no strong colour shading between different products.
  • Doing a shrinkage test on washable products as per the care instructions.
  • Checking the direction of threads (the grain line) of the main fabric and comparing it with the client's sample or pattern.
  • Checking seam strength, by pulling both sides with normal force.
  • Checking all labelling as per approved artworks, and the labelling quality and composition as per the buyer's requirement.
  • Keeping all labelling, marking, and product physical samples with us for any future order or reference.
  • Checking labelling and marking placement as per the buyer's requirement.
  • Ensuring products look good and have no wrinkles before final packaging.
  • Checking box and carton quality as per the requirement of the product weight.

We believe in business transparency and share all inspection details: labelling and marking physical-sample images, their placement, product final images, and packing images.
