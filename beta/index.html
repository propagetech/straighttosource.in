Who we are

Straight to Source operates out of New Delhi with teams across Moradabad, Jodhpur, Jaipur, Saharanpur, Kanpur and Karur — India's leading production clusters for furniture, home décor, textiles, equestrian goods and metalware.

Sourcing from India offers strong opportunities, but it needs reliable factory selection, hands-on production management, clear communication, quality discipline and export coordination. STS brings these functions together so buyers get better visibility, fewer surprises and stronger execution.

How we work

Factory shortlisting
