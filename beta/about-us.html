About Straight to Source

We work where the product is made.

A buying agency embedded in India's production clusters — not managing it from a distance, but on the factory floor, at the sample table, inside the finishing unit. That is the difference.

The story behind the name

Our background

Straight to Source was founded on a single frustration: buyers in Europe and North America were spending months chasing Indian suppliers by email, receiving shipments that differed from the samples, and having no reliable partner on the ground to hold the line.

We built the company to be that partner — a professional buying agency with real offices inside India's seven major production clusters, staffed by experienced merchandisers and trained quality inspectors who speak both languages: the buyer's requirements, and the factory's realities.

Today we work with wholesalers and retailers across Germany, the UK, France, Belgium, the Netherlands, Denmark, the USA, Canada and Australia — sourcing furniture, textiles, accessories, lighting, gifts, soft goods and equestrian equipment out of India, fully inspected and logistics-coordinated before a single carton ships.

"The gap between what a buyer orders and what arrives in the container is almost always solved on the factory floor — not in the email thread."

What makes us different

Our approach
01

Embedded, not remote

Our teams live and work inside the production clusters — Jodhpur for furniture and blue pottery, Moradabad for metalware, Saharanpur for wood carving, Kanpur for leather, Tuticorin for textiles. When a factory has a problem, we are already there.

02

One relationship, full scope

Sourcing, quality control and logistics managed under one roof and one contact. No finger-pointing between agents when something goes wrong on the production floor — we own the outcome, beginning to end.

03

Your India office

We attend trade fairs on your behalf, visit factories in your name, send you reports in your format and respond in your time zone. For most clients, we are indistinguishable from having an in-house India team — at a fraction of the overhead.

04

QC as standard, not an add-on

Every production run we manage includes quality inspection — it is not an optional bolt-on. Pre-production, in-line and pre-shipment checks are how we protect your brand at the point of origin.

05

Price discipline

Our merchandisers re-engineer products and negotiate with vendors to hit your landed cost target. We know the cluster rates, the seasonal capacity windows and the levers that move price without cutting quality.

06

B2B only

We work exclusively with wholesalers, importers and retailers — never with individual consumers. Our compliance, documentation and volume processes are built for the demands of commercial trade.
