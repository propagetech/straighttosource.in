Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Straight to Source ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us or use this website, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to share with us, such as your name, company, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, and the details of your sourcing enquiry. This website does not require you to create an account.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiries, prepare quotes and samples, manage orders, and communicate with you about your sourcing requirements. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing with third parties

We may share necessary order details with the manufacturers, inspection partners, and logistics providers required to fulfil your request. We ask these partners to handle your information responsibly.

Messaging platforms

When you contact us through WhatsApp, email, or phone, your messages are also subject to the privacy practices of those service providers.

Data retention

We keep enquiry and order information only for as long as needed to serve you and to meet our legal and accounting obligations.

Your choices

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have shared with us at any time by contacting ananya@straighttosource.in.
