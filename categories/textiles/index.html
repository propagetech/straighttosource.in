Overview

Handloom heritage, export-ready finish

India's textile clusters are among the most skilled in the world. We work directly with handloom weavers, block-print studios, and mills across Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, and beyond to bring you authentic craft at consistent, export-grade quality.

Whether you need seasonal scarf collections, specialty fabric by the metre, or fully custom weaves, we manage sampling, production, and inspection so what you order is what you receive.
