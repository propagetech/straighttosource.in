Who we are

Welcome to Straight to Source

Straight to Source (StoS) is a buying agency operating out of New Delhi, India, with satellite offices in Jodhpur, Moradabad, and Karur. StoS provides product sourcing, production management, quality control, and logistics services to buyers and importers globally.

We aim to become our buyers' eyes and ears on the ground, delivering quality Indian products within the agreed time frame.
