The studio

A studio in India.

India makes some of the most beautiful things in the world. The difficulty has never been finding them. It is standing in the workshop when it matters.

We are a small studio, embedded across India's craft capitals, sourcing and overseeing production for buyers who can't be here, but won't compromise.

How we work

Your eyes and ears, on the ground.

Most buyers source India from a desk thousands of miles away. We work the other way around. Our merchandisers sit with the makers, refine the sample, and stay through production, so the last unit looks like the first.

We negotiate the right price, re-engineer where a target demands it, and keep one honest line of communication open between the workshop and you. When something is not right, you hear it from us first.
