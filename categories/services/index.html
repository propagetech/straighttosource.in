Quality & Inspection

On-site quality control at source, pre-shipment inspection, and end-to-end logistics, so you ship with confidence.

Overview

Eyes on the floor, at every stage

Sourcing well is only half the job. Our inspectors are present at the factory, checking workmanship, measurements, and finishing against agreed standards, before goods are packed and shipped.

We run in-line and pre-shipment inspections to AQL sampling plans, coordinate lab testing where required, and manage export documentation and logistics through to your port.
