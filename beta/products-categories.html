Category 01

Furniture

India is one of the world's most significant furniture exporters, with Jodhpur and Saharanpur accounting for the majority of handcrafted wood and iron pieces going to European and North American wholesale buyers.

We source across the full range — solid wood dining and bedroom furniture, accent and occasional pieces, iron and reclaimed wood combinations, painted and distressed finishes — working with factories that have consistent export track records and the QC infrastructure to hold your standard across a full production run.

Accessories

India's metalware and home accessories sector — centred on Moradabad and Delhi — has been supplying European and American importers for over a century. The craft base is deep, the vendor range is wide, and the product diversity is exceptional.

We source decorative home accessories, tabletop objects, candle holders, frames, trays and decorative bowls across brass, iron, aluminium, zinc alloy and mixed-media finishes — from natural oxidised metal to electroplated gold and silver. Minimum quantities are flexible and suited to wholesale buyers at all scales.

Gifts

India is a natural source for giftable product — handcrafted, origin-rich and suited to the mid-to-premium gift retail market in Europe and North America. We work with vendors experienced in gift-ready packaging, sets and assortments, and seasonal collections — appropriate for buyers building range plans for Christmas, Mother's Day and gifting occasions throughout the year. Compliance with destination-market packaging and labelling regulations is managed as part of every order.

Category 04

Textiles

India's textile tradition is among the oldest and most technically varied in the world. From Rajasthan's block-printed cottons to Tamil Nadu's woven linens and Kanpur's dyed fabrications, the range available to wholesale buyers is unmatched by any other single-country sourcing destination.

We source bed linen, table linen, throws, kitchen textiles and fabric by the metre — across 100% cotton, linen blends, stonewashed, embroidered and printed constructions. All textiles are checked for colour fastness, dimensional stability and shrinkage before dispatch.

Category 05

Soft Goods

Cushions, poufs, floor cushions and upholstered storage are among India's strongest wholesale export categories — combining craft textile production with competitive sewing and finishing labour. Jaipur and Kanpur are the primary production centres, with Tuticorin handling woven outer fabrics.

We source filled and unfilled constructions to your specification — cover fabric, fill material, zipper type, label, packaging and carton configuration all managed as part of the brief. Inner fillings can be specified as hollow fibre, wool, cotton or kapok depending on your market requirements.

Category 06

Equestrian

Kanpur has been India's leather capital for over a hundred years, home to a tannery and finished goods manufacturing base that supplies equestrian and saddlery markets worldwide. The leather quality, at the price point that Kanpur delivers, is difficult to match anywhere else.

We source saddlery, riding accessories and stable equipment from established Kanpur manufacturers with direct export experience to EU and UK equestrian buyers. All leather goods are checked for stitching integrity, hardware quality and finish consistency. REACH compliance is managed as standard for EU-bound goods.

Category 07

Lighting

Moradabad and Saharanpur produce a wide range of decorative lighting — from iron and brass pendant fixtures to carved wooden table lamps and woven rattan shades — that has found a consistent market with European interior retailers and home décor wholesalers for decades.

We source pendant lights, table lamps, floor lamps, wall sconces and decorative lanterns across metal, wood, rattan and glass constructions. Electrical components can be specified for EU (CE), UK or US (UL) standards. We coordinate electrical compliance testing and certification for all wired fittings as part of the order process.
