What we do

We bridge the gap between global buyers and Indian manufacturing.
Sourcing from India offers strong opportunities, but it also needs reliable factory selection, hands-on production management, clear communication, quality discipline and export coordination. STS brings these functions together so buyers get better visibility, fewer surprises and stronger execution.

How we work

A clear operating rhythm for every project.
01. Brief
We understand your product, target cost, compliance needs, volume, timeline and quality expectations.

02. Match
We shortlist suitable factories based on category capability, scale, price, workmanship and reliability.

03. Develop
We coordinate samples, finishes, packaging, technical changes and approval documentation.

04. Manage
We track production progress, flag risks early and keep factories aligned with your shipment plan.

05. Inspect
We perform structured inspections and share practical reports with photos, findings and required actions.

06. Ship
We support export documentation, factory dispatch coordination and shipment readiness until handover.
