What we do · 01

Sourcing & production.

The right maker, the right price, and someone who stays in the workshop until the last unit looks like the first.

We choose makers on capability and conscience — can they make it well, and will they make it right.

Finding the maker

The right manufacturer, vetted in person.

We evaluate factories on their capability to make your product and their commitment to quality, safety, and compliance, then negotiate a price that holds. Where a target demands it, we re-engineer the product with the maker rather than cut a corner.

Throughout, we are the constant link between workshop and buyer, keeping the order moving and you informed.

  • Factory evaluation
  • Price negotiation
  • Sampling & development
  • Re-engineering to target

Quality, step by step

Checked at every stage, not just the last.

Quality is the one thing a buyer can't verify from afar, so we verify it for you. We inspect at initial, mid-line, and final stages, right from order placement until the goods leave the factory floor.

You receive a clear, photographic account at each step, so there are no surprises when the container is opened.

  • Initial inspection
  • Mid-line inspection
  • Final inspection
  • Photographic reports
