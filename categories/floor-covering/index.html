Floor Covering

Floor covering is material made from textiles, felts, resins, rubber, or other natural or man-made substances, laid upon the base surface of a room to provide comfort, durability, safety, and decoration. It includes both handmade and machine-made rugs and carpets and smooth-surfaced floor coverings.
