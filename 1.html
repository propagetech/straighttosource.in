Who we are

Straight to Source operates out of New Delhi with teams across Jodhpur, Jaipur, Moradabad, Saharanpur, Tuticorin and Kanpur — India's leading production clusters for furniture, textiles, equestrian goods and metalware. We sit between your design brief and the factory floor, managing vendor selection, price negotiation, production timelines and quality before a single carton leaves the country.

Our merchandisers re-engineer products to hit your price targets, our QC inspectors hold the line on the standard you ordered to, and we stay in contact with the freight forwarder until the bill of lading is in your inbox.
