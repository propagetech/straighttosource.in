About us

Global Sourcing, Simplified

A decade of connecting global buyers directly with the makers behind the products.

Our story

Built on trust, made by hand

Straight to Source was founded with a simple mission: to connect global buyers directly with quality manufacturers and suppliers. We understand the challenges of international sourcing, from language barriers and quality inconsistencies to logistics complexity.

With over a decade of experience in global supply chain management, we've built a network of trusted suppliers across textiles, jewellery, bags, and handcrafted home goods. Our team personally vets every supplier to ensure they meet international quality standards.

Today, we serve importers and distributors worldwide, helping them source products faster, with greater confidence, and at competitive prices.
