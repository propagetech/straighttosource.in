What we do · 02

Quality & inspection.

We are your eyes on the ground. Trained QC inspectors hold production to your standard, stage by stage, with a full photographic account.

We inspect as if the goods were our own. Nothing ships on hope.

The report

You see what we see.

Every inspection comes back as a clear, photographed record: what was checked, what passed, what didn't, and what happened next. It is the closest thing to standing on the factory floor yourself.

We check at initial, mid-line, and final stages, so problems are caught while they can still be fixed, not after the container has sailed.

  • Initial · mid-line · final
  • Photographic reporting
  • Defect classification
  • Pre-shipment sign-off
